Welcome to Monkey Temple Homestay

Greetings and Namaste Travelers,

Monkey Temple Home Stay is delighted to welcome people from different parts of the world to this beautiful Himalayan nation Nepal. Come and enjoy the splendors of nature and richest culture of Nepal. We offer you blissful moments while hosting your stay at our family home. Ethnic Tamang Family will ensure your safety and authentic information about Nepalese culture, traditions and rituals. We always believe that visitors are deities and you too will be treated as our special visitor. Feel at home and experience Nepalese way of living a life. Hygienic food and clean accommodation facilities, traditional & modern amenities along with stunning view of famous MonkeyTemple (Swyambhunath) from room window will definitely make your stay in our home memorable.

Amenities

Home Stay concept is all about experiencing the local way of living while holidaying in unknown places and mingling with the locals. This can present awkward moments of uncomfortable circumstances. However, Monkey Temple Home Stay has tried to endow better upgraded facilities to its visitors in the same way provide authentic local rites, culture and traditions. Therefore those who choose us for their stay in Kathmandu will get both traditional Nepalese styles of living as well as modern facilities.
