About Us



The host family has four members. Lapsang Lama is a keen admirer of traveling and he himself travels a lot. Therefore, understanding of travelling essence makes your stay at his house very comfortable. Aged 48 years Mr. Lama is very aesthetic personnel with lots of travel tales to share with you. Born in remote region of Temal Kavrepalanchok -4 Boldephediche village Mr. Lama is from the Tamang ethnic community of Nepal.

These people follow Buddhism and also practice some of the celebrations of Hinduism too. Adored by society and loved by his wife Ms. Santu Maya Lama (43 years old), daughter (Chhuyang Dong Tamang) and son (Chhupten Dong Tamang) Mr. Lama is considered as the most humble person in the society. Experience the remote facts of Nepal’s hilly civilization while you stay at his home.

Ms. Santu Maya Lama is a typical Nepali woman with very expert kitchen skills.

She is very famous for her kind gesture towards her colleagues and friends. Her cooking skills are very genuine and you can relish various local delicacies. She is an anchor of the Lama family’s house. Her smiling personality will definitely rub positive vibes on any visitors staying at the house.

Together with this beautiful ideal couple two kids will make you feel at home. Be dignified happy guests of Lama Family and take back alluring moments of spending time in Nepal.
